Cheesecake Recipe

Here's a cheesecake recipe. I've made this several times now and had really tasty results each time!

Ingredients:

  • Packet of digestives
  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 900g soft cheese
  • 225g sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C or gasmark 4
  2. Crush the packet of digestives into crumbs
  3. Melt 100g butter and mix it into the crumbs
  4. Spread the crumbs evenly onto the base of a cake tin
  5. Whisk 900g of soft cheese with 225g of granulated sugar until combined
  6. Add 4 eggs while whisking and then a teaspoon of vanilla extract
  7. Pour the mix into the cake tin over the crumb base
  8. Wrap the cake tin in foil and place in a tray of hot water before transferring to the oven. Make sure to use 2 layers of foil to avoid a soggy bottom
  9. Allow to bake for 1 hour or until top of cheesecake is golden brown

To make the coulis:

Ingredients:

  • 100g berries of your choice
  • Juice of one lemon
  • Sugar to taste

Instructions:

  1. Simmer the berries in the lemon juice while mashing with a fork until liquified
  2. Gradually add sugar, tasting as you go
  3. When you reach your preferred sweetness, strain the coulis into a jar for later
